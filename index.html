Our Story

Born in a unconscious thought
Perfected in a Kitchen.

It started at a random walk down Kabalagala to get something nice to eat after campus in 2024. Couldn't find anything not until I went back home and then remebered my time in Korea when I tried once a Corndog. Two years of obsessive recipe development later, ONtheStick was born with one mission: make the Kampala's most extraordinary corndog.

We source HALAL certifed meats from family farms. Our cornmeal is also halal certified and got from trusted companies. Every sauce is made from scratch each morning. Nothing is frozen. Nothing is rushed.
